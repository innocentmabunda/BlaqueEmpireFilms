What makes BLAQUE EMPIRE FILMS special?


Blaque Empire Films (pty) Ltd was established in 2016 and it is 100% Black Owned Company founded by Kgaitsedi Mabunda and Richard Mohlari.

The focus of the company is to film reality TV shows and movies. Blaque Empire Films has, to date, filmed a few corporate events and all were successfully delivered and received.
